Funko POPs!

Funko Pop! is a line of small (about 4 inches tall) vinyl figures that portray some of the most popular characters ever created, such as Iron Man, Spider-Man, and Hulk. Their small size and cutesy designs make them great for collectors and casual fans alike.

Here at Z's Comic Lair we have a ton of Funko POPs! for sale! We have a little bit of everything from Sci-Fi all the way to Anime.
